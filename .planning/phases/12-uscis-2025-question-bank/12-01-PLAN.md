**Step 1: Extend Question type in `src/types/index.ts`**

Add `DynamicAnswerMeta` interface and optional `dynamic` field to the `Question` interface:

```typescript
export interface DynamicAnswerMeta {
  /** 'time' = changes with elections/appointments; 'state' = varies by user's location */
  type: 'time' | 'state';
  /** Semantic field name, e.g. 'president', 'senators', 'governor', 'capital' */
  field: string;
  /** ISO date when this answer was last verified, e.g. '2026-02-09' */
  lastVerified: string;
  /** Human-readable trigger for when to update, e.g. 'presidential election (every 4 years)' */
  updateTrigger: string;
}
```

Add to `Question` interface:
```typescript
  /** Marks questions whose answers change over time or vary by location */
  dynamic?: DynamicAnswerMeta;
```

2. **GOV-S40** - "Why do U.S. representatives serve shorter terms than U.S. senators?"
   - Category: `'System of Government'`
   - Study answers: To more closely follow public opinion, to be more responsive to the people
   - Distractors: Because they have less power, Because the Constitution was amended, Because they represent fewer people

3. **GOV-S41** - "How many senators does each state have?"
   - Category: `'System of Government'`
   - Study answer: Two (2)
   - Distractors: One (1), Four (4), It depends on the state's population

4. **GOV-S42** - "Why does each state have two senators?"
   - Category: `'System of Government'`
   - Study answers: Equal representation for small and large states, so all states have equal power in the Senate
   - Distractors: Because it was George Washington's idea, Because there are two parties, Because senators serve two terms

5. **GOV-S43** - "The President of the United States can serve only two terms. Why?"
   - Category: `'System of Government'`
   - Study answers: Because of the 22nd Amendment, to prevent abuse of power, to ensure peaceful transfer of power
   - Distractors: Because of the Bill of Rights, Because of the Supreme Court, Because Congress limits it

6. **GOV-S44** - "Why is the Electoral College important?"
   - Category: `'System of Government'`
   - Study answers: It decides who is elected president, it provides a compromise between the popular vote and congressional vote
   - Distractors: It trains future politicians, It sets the budget, It approves new laws

7. **GOV-S45** - "How long do Supreme Court justices serve?"
   - Category: `'System of Government'`
   - Study answers: They are appointed for life, until they want to retire, until they die, until they are impeached and convicted
   - Distractors: 20 years, 10 years, Until the next president takes office

8. **GOV-S46** - "Why do Supreme Court justices serve for life?"
   - Category: `'System of Government'`
   - Study answers: To be independent from politics, so they can make decisions based on the Constitution rather than popular opinion
   - Distractors: Because they are elected by the people, Because the President decides, Because Congress cannot remove them
